Paper Presentation

Paper presentation is one of most important event which is defines an engineer by igniting your presentation skills. It is a way to share your research or ideas with an audience. You typically prepare a written paper and then present it in front of a group of people. It's like giving a mini-lecture where you explain your topic, discuss your findings or arguments, and answer questions from the audience.

Rules and Guidelines

  • A team should consist maximum of 3 members.
  • College ID is mandatory.
  • It is not necessary that the participants forming a team should be from the same college.
  • No participant can be part of more than one team.
  • On spot entries are also accepted.
  • Teams should wait till the winners announcement.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and up for no discussion.

Paper Rules

  • The paper should strictly follow IEEE format and the paper should be in pdf format.
  • Abstract should not exceed the limit of 200 words.
  • The Abstract must be shared on or before 13/03/2024 to the mail ID inmatrix24.paperpresentation@gmail.com
  • Font style for text should be 'Times New Roman'.
  • Font size for the heading must be 26 and for the sub heading should be 24.
  • Font size for text should be 18 and margin of 1 inch for all sides is must.
  • Plagiarism is strictly prohibited and citing the references is mandatory.
  • The front page should have the title of the paper on the top and the details of the team members at the bottom.
  • The Abstract should also be attached with ppt.
  • The Paper must be shared on or before 14/03/2024 to the mail ID inmatrix24.paperpresentation@gmail.com

Presentation Rules

  • Each team will be given 7 to10 minutes for the presentation.
  • Do not use more than 2 font colours per slide.
  • The final paper must be shared 2 days before the actual day of the event to the mail ID immatrix24.paperpresentation@gmail.com
  • Violation of any rule can result in rejection of paper.
