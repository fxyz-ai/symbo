Paper Presentation

Paper presentation is one of most important event which is defines an engineer by igniting your presentation skills. It is a way to share your research or ideas with an audience. You typically prepare a written paper and then present it in front of a group of people. It's like giving a mini-lecture where you explain your topic, discuss your findings or arguments, and answer questions from the audience.

Domains

  • Internet Of Things
  • Machine Learning
  • Data Analytics
  • Cyber Security
  • Cloud Computing
  • BlockChain Technology
  • Image Processing
  • Augmented Reality / Virtual Reality
  • Networking
  • Big Data Analytics
  • Robotics

Rules and Guidelines

  • A team should consist maximum of 3 members.
  • College ID is mandatory.
  • It is not necessary that the participants forming a team should be from the same college.
  • No participant can be part of more than one team.
  • On the spot entries are also accepted.
  • Teams should be in the venue till the event finishes over with the winners announcement.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and up for no discussion.
