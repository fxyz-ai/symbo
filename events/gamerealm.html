Rules and Guidelines

  • The game will begin with a classic match for all teams, from which the top 4 teams will advance.
  • Following this, a TDM match will be held among these 4 teams, with the winners from each team progressing.
  • Subsequently, the last 2 teams will face off in a final TDM match to determine the ultimate victor.
