Pitchdeck

Life becomes easier and better by our ideas and innovation. Startup ideas are a great platform to expose your ideas for the betterment of the future. A startup idea is a concept for a new business venture that aims to solve a problem or meet a need in the market.

Rules and Guidelines

  • The team, consisting of at least two and up to four members.
  • They should present ideas using PowerPoint presentations at the first level, followed by prototyping if feasible at the second level within a time frame of 5 to 10 minutes.
  • The team should have a name.
  • Evaluation will be conducted offline manually.
