WizHack

Good programmers analyze the problems and finds the solution and codes it. This event aims for a healthy competition to find the best and most efficient code. This is like being a detective in the digital world. It's the process of finding and fixing errors or bugs in computer programs.

Rules and Guidelines

  • The event will take place over 2 rounds, participants are supposed to compete individually.
  • Round 1 will last for 30mins and participants can attend it anytime before the deadline.
  • For round 2 participants are requested to be present at the venue on time.
  • Verification and briefing will take place a few mins before you can start the challenge, so plan accordingly and bring your ID.
  • Your workspace will consist of a lab PC, no other external aids are allowed.
  • There will be flexibility in choosing preferred questions, which can range from debugging existing code to providing your own.
  • Challenges are curated to mainly focus on problem solving skills in c/c++/java/python, but knowledge in other domains will come in handy.
  • Evaluation criteria is based on a score system, which will be determined by the difficult of the problem, time taken and other factors.
  • Every question will have hints, which can be used at the cost of negatively impacting your points.
  • Communication between participants is prohibited, for any queries contact the nearest available coordinator.
